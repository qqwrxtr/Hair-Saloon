We're excited to announce our exclusive Summer Styling Workshop series, happening this June at our Maida Vale salon. This hands-on masterclass is your opportunity to learn professional styling techniques directly from Equipe Hair's award-winning stylists.

Whether you struggle with creating the perfect beachy waves, can't quite nail that salon-worthy blowout, or simply want to expand your styling repertoire for the summer season, this workshop is designed to transform your skills and boost your confidence.

Limited to just 8 participants per session, this intimate workshop ensures plenty of personal attention and guidance as you master new techniques under expert supervision.

Workshop Details

Event Information

  • Dates: June 11, 18, and 25, 2025
  • Time: 6:30 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Location: Equipe Hair Maida Vale, 9 Clifton Road, London W9 1SZ
  • Group Size: 8 participants maximum per session
  • Price: £95 per person (includes refreshments and a styling product kit worth £45)

Each workshop date will focus on the same techniques, so you only need to attend one session. If you're interested in multiple styling approaches, we recommend booking our comprehensive full-day master class in September (details coming soon).

What You'll Learn

This summer-focused workshop has been carefully designed to help you create versatile styles that withstand heat, humidity, and active summer days. Our expert instructors will guide you through:

1. Summer-Proof Blowout Techniques

  • How to achieve volume and bounce that lasts in summer humidity
  • Quick-drying techniques to minimize heat exposure
  • Products and tools that enhance longevity in warm weather

2. Beach Waves Masterclass

  • Different techniques for creating various wave patterns
  • How to customize beach waves for different hair lengths and textures
  • Heat-free overnight methods for effortless waves

3. Summer Updos and Half-Up Styles

  • Quick and easy updos for hot days that still look polished
  • Versatile half-up styles that transition from day to evening
  • How to incorporate accessories for added flair

4. Styling Product Masterclass

  • Understanding which products work best for your hair type
  • Proper application techniques for maximum effectiveness
  • Creating a personalized summer styling product routine
"Our goal is to demystify professional styling techniques and empower you to recreate salon-quality looks at home. We'll break down complex methods into simple, repeatable steps that work for your specific hair type and lifestyle." — James Wilson, Style Director

Meet Your Instructors

Learn from our team of award-winning stylists who bring years of salon, editorial, and runway experience:

James Wilson

Style Director

With over 15 years of experience, James has styled hair for London Fashion Week and numerous editorial shoots. He specializes in creating effortless, wearable styles with a focus on enhancing natural texture.

Sophia Martinez

Color Specialist

Sophia brings a unique perspective on how color and styling work together. Her expertise in creating dimensional color has made her a sought-after educator in the industry, teaching stylists across the UK.

Both instructors will be present throughout the workshop, giving you access to their combined expertise and allowing for more personalized attention.

Who Should Attend

This workshop is perfect for:

  • Styling Beginners: If you struggle with basic styling techniques and want to build confidence
  • Intermediate Stylists: Those who can create basic styles but want to refine techniques and expand their repertoire
  • Anyone Planning Summer Events: Perfect for those with weddings, vacations, or special occasions this summer
  • People with Specific Hair Challenges: If you find your hair particularly difficult to style in summer humidity or heat

The techniques taught are adaptable for all hair types and lengths, though some modifications may be necessary for very short hair or highly textured hair. Our instructors will provide personalized guidance based on your specific hair characteristics.

"We've designed this workshop to be accessible for all skill levels. Even if you've never picked up a curling iron before, you'll leave with the confidence to create beautiful styles at home." — Olivia Taylor, Event Coordinator

What to Bring

To make the most of your workshop experience, please bring:

  • Your own styling tools (if you have them) - this allows you to learn on the equipment you'll be using at home
  • Any styling products you currently use and enjoy
  • A notebook for taking notes (though we'll provide a detailed handout)
  • A water bottle (we'll provide additional refreshments)

Note: If you don't own styling tools yet, don't worry! We'll have professional tools available for you to use during the workshop, and our experts can help guide you on which tools might be best for your specific needs before you invest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have experience with hair styling?

Not at all! Our workshop is designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to those looking to refine their techniques.

Will the techniques work for my hair type?

Yes, our instructors will demonstrate techniques that can be adapted for different hair types and lengths. They'll provide personalized advice for your specific hair characteristics.

What if I don't own styling tools?

We'll have professional tools available for everyone to use during the workshop. This is actually a great opportunity to try different tools before investing in your own.

Will I be styling my own hair or working on a mannequin?

You'll be styling your own hair with guidance from our instructors. This gives you the most realistic practice and allows you to learn techniques specifically for your hair.

What if I need to change my booking date?

We understand plans change! Contact us at least 48 hours before your booked session, and we'll do our best to accommodate a date change, subject to availability.

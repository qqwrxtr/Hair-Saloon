Summer Hair Trends You Need to Try in 2025

Color Director

Summer is just around the corner, and it's the perfect time to refresh your look with a trendy new hairstyle. The summer of 2025 is bringing exciting hair trends that combine effortless style with bold statements. Whether you're looking for a complete transformation or a subtle update, there's something for everyone this season.

At Equipe Hair, we're always at the forefront of hair trends, constantly updating our techniques and knowledge to bring you the most current styles. Our team has been busy researching, attending industry events, and experimenting with new looks to bring you this curated list of the hottest summer hair trends for 2025.

Trend 1: Copper and Cinnamon Tones

Warm, rich copper and cinnamon tones are taking center stage this summer. These vibrant red-orange hues add warmth to your complexion and create a stunning sun-kissed effect that's perfect for summer.

The beauty of this trend lies in its versatility—copper can be adjusted to complement any skin tone. For fair skin, a lighter strawberry copper creates a beautiful contrast. Medium skin tones shine with true copper, while deeper skin tones glow with rich auburn-copper blends.

To maintain this vibrant color:

  • Use color-safe, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Rinse with cool water to seal the cuticle and preserve color
  • Apply a UV protectant spray before sun exposure
  • Schedule regular toning appointments every 4-6 weeks

Trend 2: Modern Shag with Curtain Bangs

The shag haircut continues to evolve, and this summer's version features soft layers combined with face-framing curtain bangs. This low-maintenance cut adds volume, texture, and movement—perfect for those hot summer days when you want to wash and go.

What makes the modern shag so appealing is its adaptability to different hair types and face shapes. The layering can be customized to enhance your natural texture, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

To style your modern shag:

  • Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to damp hair
  • Diffuse or air dry to enhance natural texture
  • Use a flat iron to create subtle bends in the curtain bangs
  • Finish with a lightweight texturizing paste for piece-y definition
"The modern shag is all about embracing your natural texture and creating effortless movement. It's the perfect summer cut because it actually looks better a bit tousled and undone." — Emma Roberts, Color Director

Trend 3: Lived-In Blonde

Gone are the days of high-maintenance, perfectly highlighted blonde. Summer 2025 is all about the "lived-in" blonde—a more natural, dimensional approach to blonde hair that features darker roots blending seamlessly into lighter ends.

This technique combines balayage, babylights, and strategic placement of highlights to create a sun-kissed effect that mimics how hair naturally lightens in the summer. The result is a low-maintenance color that grows out beautifully, requiring fewer touch-ups.

Benefits of lived-in blonde include:

  • Less damage due to fewer full-head bleaching sessions
  • Reduced maintenance and salon visits
  • Dimension and depth that flatters all skin tones
  • Natural-looking grow-out phase

Trend 4: Bob Variations

The bob haircut continues to reign supreme, but this summer brings new variations that keep this classic cut fresh and exciting. From the sliced bob to the Italian bob, there's a version for every face shape and personal style.

Popular Bob Variations for Summer 2025:

The Sliced Bob: Features razor-cut ends that create movement and texture without layers. Perfect for fine to medium hair types.

The Italian Bob: A voluminous, slightly rounded bob with soft layers that frame the face. Ideal for those who want volume and softness.

The Asymmetric Bob: Slightly longer on one side, this modern take adds an edge to the classic silhouette.

The Collarbone Bob: Hitting right at the collarbone, this longer version is perfect for those who want versatility in styling.

Trend 5: Beach Waves 2.0

Beach waves have been a summer staple for years, but 2025 brings an evolution of this beloved style. The new beach waves are more natural, slightly messier, and less uniform than their predecessors.

This updated version creates texture that looks like you've spent the day at the beach—tousled, effortless, and full of movement. The key difference is that these waves vary in size and direction, mimicking how hair naturally waves after a swim in the ocean.

To achieve the perfect beach waves:

  • Start with a salt spray or texture spray on damp hair
  • Twist sections away from the face while blow-drying
  • Use a 1.25-inch curling iron on random sections, leaving the ends straight
  • Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look
  • Break up the curls with your fingers and finish with a dry texturizing spray

How to Choose the Right Trend for You

With so many exciting trends to choose from, how do you decide which one is right for you? Consider these factors when making your decision:

Face Shape

Different cuts and styles flatter different face shapes. For example, curtain bangs work well on oval, heart, and square faces, while asymmetric bobs can balance out round face shapes.

Hair Texture

Work with your natural texture rather than against it. The modern shag, for instance, is fantastic for wavy and curly hair, while sliced bobs work beautifully on straighter hair types.

Lifestyle and Maintenance

Be honest about how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day. Lived-in blonde and modern shags are lower maintenance, while some bob styles might require more frequent trimming.

Skin Tone

When considering color trends like copper or lived-in blonde, your skin's undertones play a crucial role. Warm skin tones often pair beautifully with copper, while lived-in blonde can be customized for any skin tone.

Conclusion

Summer 2025 is all about embracing natural texture, low-maintenance color, and versatile cuts that can be styled multiple ways. Whether you opt for the warmth of copper tones, the effortless cool of a modern shag, or the timeless chic of a bob variation, the key is to choose a trend that makes you feel confident and suits your lifestyle.

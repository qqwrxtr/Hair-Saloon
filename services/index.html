Special Occasion FAQ

For weddings, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, with a trial run 1-2 months before the event. For other special occasions, booking 2-4 weeks ahead is advised, especially during peak seasons (prom, holiday parties, etc.).

For most updo styles, day-old hair works best as it has more grip. We recommend washing your hair the day before your appointment and avoiding heavy conditioners or styling products. If needed, we can always wash your hair at the salon.

Bring any hair accessories or pieces you plan to wear (veil, hairpins, flowers, etc.). We also recommend bringing photos of styles you like for inspiration. For wedding trials, bringing a photo of your dress can help us create a complementary style.

Special occasion styling typically takes 45-90 minutes, depending on the complexity of the style and your hair length/thickness. For wedding days or large parties, we'll create a detailed timeline to ensure everyone is ready on time.

Extension Care

Proper maintenance is essential for keeping your extensions looking beautiful and lasting as long as possible. We provide detailed care instructions and recommend the following:

  • Use sulfate-free, extension-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Brush gently with an extension-specific brush
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair
  • Schedule regular maintenance appointments

Extension FAQs

How long do extensions last?

Depending on the type and your hair growth, extensions typically last 6-12 weeks before needing maintenance.

Will extensions damage my hair?

When applied by our skilled professionals and properly maintained, extensions should not cause damage to your natural hair.

Can I color my extensions?

We recommend professional coloring of extensions only. Human hair extensions can be colored, but synthetic cannot.

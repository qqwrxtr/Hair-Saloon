About Equipe Hair

Discover the story behind London's premier hair salon and the team dedicated to making you look and feel your best.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by award-winning stylist Emma Richardson, Equipe Hair began as a small boutique salon in Maida Vale with a vision to create a space where hair styling was treated as an art form and clients received personalized, luxurious service.

Over the years, we've grown into one of London's most respected salons, expanding to our Finchley location in 2012, but never losing sight of our original philosophy: to make every client feel special and to deliver exceptional results that enhance their natural beauty.

Today, Equipe Hair is home to a team of talented stylists who share a passion for creativity, continuous learning, and client satisfaction. We pride ourselves on staying at the forefront of hair trends and techniques while maintaining the warm, personalized service that has been our hallmark since day one.

Our Philosophy

At Equipe Hair, we believe in more than just cutting and styling hair. We're guided by core principles that define everything we do.

Personalized Approach

We believe that no two clients are the same. Every service begins with a thorough consultation to understand your unique style, preferences, and lifestyle, ensuring results that are perfectly tailored to you.

Excellence in Craft

We're committed to mastery in hair styling. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay at the cutting edge of techniques and trends, ensuring we deliver exceptional results with every service.

Care & Connection

Beyond technical skill, we value the relationships we build with our clients. We strive to create a warm, welcoming environment where you can relax, be yourself, and trust that you're in good hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Equipe Hair Salon. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us.

While we do accept walk-ins when possible, we strongly recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred stylist, date, and time. Our popular stylists are often booked several weeks in advance, especially for weekend appointments.

Our pricing varies depending on the service, the length and thickness of your hair, and the level of stylist you choose. We offer a range of options to suit different budgets. Please visit our Services page for detailed pricing information or call us for a personalized quote.

We use only premium, professional-grade products that deliver exceptional results while caring for your hair. Our main product lines include Kérastase, Olaplex, Kevin Murphy, and Wella Professionals. We also offer a selection of these products for purchase to maintain your style at home.

Your first visit will begin with a thorough consultation where your stylist will discuss your hair goals, lifestyle, and preferences. We'll analyze your hair type and face shape to recommend the best options for you. We encourage you to bring reference photos of styles you like. After the consultation, you'll enjoy our signature service, which includes a relaxing shampoo experience and expert styling.
